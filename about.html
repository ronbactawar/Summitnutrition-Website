About Me

Background

Basil Bactawar is owner/manager of Roraima Feeds. His education background includes a B.Sc. in Agriculture, a M.Sc. in Animal Science a Certificate in International Development. Basil Bactawar has also helped feed manufacturers in nine developing countries to formulate feed. Most notably, he has received an international award from the University of Florida on his work that assisted overseas manufacturers and farmers during the last 18 years.

Additionally, Basil Bactawar has extensive experience in agricultural extension. He has been working in Agricultural Extension for about 40 years. He started his career in Guyana with the Ministry of Agriculture where he served as Livestock Officer (Forage Agronomy and Animal Nutrition) for 7 years. He later worked in Canada with the British Columbia Ministry of Agriculture as a Livestock Industry Development Specialist for 16 years. He moved to Florida on a job offer and worked as a County Extension Director with University of Florida Extension Services for 6 years. He trained farmers in fourteen countries and taught at the Guyana School of Agriculture, University of Guyana and Assiniboine Community College.
